Title: Deleting your account and data

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. It removes your
profile, saved settings, and all content you created. Before you can delete,
any active paid subscription must be cancelled first.

To delete, go to Settings > Account > Delete account and confirm with your
password. We fully erase your personal data within 30 days, except records we
are legally required to retain for tax or fraud-prevention purposes. Because
deletion is irreversible, requests that combine deletion with a refund are
reviewed by a support agent before anything is removed.
