Title: Understanding your billing and charges

Your subscription renews automatically on the same calendar day each cycle —
monthly or annually, depending on the plan you chose. We attempt the charge on
the renewal date and, if it succeeds, email you a receipt. Every invoice, paid or
failed, is available under Billing > Invoices, where you can download a PDF for
expense reports.

On your bank or card statement the charge appears as "WIDGETPRO SUBSCRIPTION"
followed by the plan name. If you see a charge you do not recognize, the most
common explanation is that a free trial recently ended: the first paid charge
lands the day after the trial period closes. The second most common is an annual
plan renewing a year after signup, which is easy to forget.

To change the card we bill, go to Billing > Payment methods, add the new card,
and set it as default; the next renewal uses it automatically. If a payment
fails — for example an expired card or insufficient funds — we retry it several
times over about 7 days and email you each time. If all retries fail, the
subscription is paused (not deleted) until you update the payment method, after
which access is restored immediately.

Prices are shown exclusive of tax where applicable; sales tax or VAT is added at
checkout based on your billing address, and the tax amount is itemized on every
invoice. If your organization is tax-exempt, add your exemption or VAT ID under
Billing > Tax information so future invoices are adjusted.
