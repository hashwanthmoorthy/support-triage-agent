Title: Understanding your billing and charges

Your subscription renews automatically on the same day each month or year,
depending on the plan you chose. The charge appears on your statement as
"WIDGETPRO SUBSCRIPTION". You can view every past invoice under Billing >
Invoices in your account.

If you see an unexpected charge, first check whether a free trial recently
ended, since the first paid charge lands the day after the trial. To update the
card we bill, go to Billing > Payment methods. Failed payments are retried for
up to 7 days before the subscription is paused.
