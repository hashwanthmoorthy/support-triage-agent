Title: Technical troubleshooting: app crashes, freezes, and performance

If the WidgetPro app crashes on launch, freezes, or runs slowly, most issues are
fixed by a few standard steps before anything else is needed. First, fully close
and reopen the app — not just minimize it — so it starts a fresh session. If it
still misbehaves, restart your device to clear memory and background processes
that can interfere.

Make sure you are on the latest version. Outdated builds are the most common
cause of crashes after a service update, because the app and server can fall out
of sync. Check for updates in your app store (mobile) or via Help > Check for
updates (desktop), install any available update, and relaunch. If a crash began
right after you updated, note the version number when you report it.

Corrupted local data can cause freezes and slow performance. Clearing the app's
cache from Settings > Storage > Clear cache is safe and does not delete your
account content, which lives on the server. As a last resort, uninstall and
reinstall the app; your data syncs back down after you sign in. On desktop,
antivirus or firewall software occasionally blocks the app — add an exception if
crashes coincide with security-software activity.

Performance problems are sometimes connectivity, not the app. A weak or
intermittent network makes the app appear frozen while it waits on the server;
switch networks or toggle airplane mode to confirm. If crashes persist after all
of this, contact support with your app version, device and OS version, and the
steps that reproduce the crash. If the app can still open, enabling Help > Send
diagnostic report attaches crash logs that let us pinpoint the cause quickly.
