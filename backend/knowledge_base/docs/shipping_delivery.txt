Title: Shipping and delivery times

Physical WidgetPro hardware ships within 2 business days of your order. Standard
shipping takes 3 to 5 business days within the continental US; express shipping
takes 1 to 2 business days. International orders take 7 to 14 business days and
may be subject to customs fees.

You will receive a tracking number by email once your order leaves our
warehouse. If your tracking has not updated in more than 5 business days, the
package may be delayed in transit and you should contact support so we can open
a carrier investigation or send a replacement.
