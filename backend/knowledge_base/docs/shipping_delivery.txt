Title: Shipping and delivery

Orders for physical WidgetPro hardware are processed and leave our warehouse
within 2 business days. Once an order ships you receive a confirmation email with
a tracking number; you can also see live status any time under Orders > Track
shipment in your account.

Delivery time depends on the method chosen at checkout. Standard shipping takes 3
to 5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping takes 1 to 2
business days. International orders take 7 to 14 business days and may be held
briefly by customs; any import duties or taxes charged by the destination country
are the recipient's responsibility and are not included in our shipping fee.

If tracking has not updated for more than 5 business days, the package may be
delayed or lost in transit. Contact support and we will open an investigation
with the carrier; if the package cannot be located we will send a free
replacement or issue a refund for the item. Packages marked delivered but not
received should be reported within 7 days so we can file a claim.

You can change the shipping address only while the order still shows "processing"
— go to Orders, select the order, and choose "Edit address". Once an order has
shipped the address is locked and you must arrange redirection with the carrier
using your tracking number. We are not able to reroute a package that has already
left the warehouse.
