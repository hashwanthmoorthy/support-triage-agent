Title: Keeping your account secure

We strongly recommend turning on two-factor authentication (2FA) under Settings >
Security. With 2FA enabled, signing in requires both your password and a code
from your authenticator app, so a stolen password alone cannot unlock your
account.

If you think your account has been accessed by someone else, change your
password immediately and sign out of all other sessions from Settings >
Security > Active sessions. We will never ask for your password or 2FA codes by
email or phone, so treat any such request as a phishing attempt and report it.
