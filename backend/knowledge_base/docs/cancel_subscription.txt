Title: Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Billing > Plan > Cancel
subscription. Cancellation stops all future charges immediately, but your paid
features stay active until the end of the current billing period — you do not
lose access you have already paid for, and you are not charged again. We send a
confirmation email as soon as the cancellation is recorded.

Cancelling is not the same as deleting your account. Cancelling only ends the
paid plan and moves you to the free tier at the end of the period, keeping your
account, data, and settings intact. If you want your account and personal data
removed entirely, follow the account deletion process instead — note that
deletion is permanent and requires cancelling any active plan first.

If you cancel by mistake or change your mind, you can reactivate before the
period ends and nothing changes; if the period has already lapsed, simply
resubscribe and your data will still be there. Reactivating an annual plan starts
a new term.

Cancellation on its own does not trigger a refund for the time already paid. If
you are within the 30-day money-back window and want your money back rather than
just stopping renewals, request a refund (see the refund policy) in addition to
cancelling. For annual plans, cancelling stops the next year's charge but the
current year is not prorated unless you are inside the 30-day window.
