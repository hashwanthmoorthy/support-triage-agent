Title: Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Billing > Plan > Cancel
subscription. Cancellation stops all future charges but keeps your account
active until the end of the current billing period, so you do not lose access
you already paid for.

Cancelling is different from deleting your account. Cancelling only ends the
paid plan and moves you to the free tier. If you want your account and personal
data removed entirely, see the account deletion policy. Cancellations take
effect immediately and you will receive a confirmation email.
