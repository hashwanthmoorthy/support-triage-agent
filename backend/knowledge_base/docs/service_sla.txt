Title: Service uptime and support response times

WidgetPro targets 99.9% monthly uptime for all paid plans. Current and
historical uptime is published on our public status page, where you can also
subscribe to incident notifications by email.

Support response times depend on your plan. Free-tier users receive a response
within 2 business days. Pro-plan users receive priority support with a target
first response within 4 business hours. Critical outages affecting many
customers are worked on immediately, regardless of plan, until service is
restored.
