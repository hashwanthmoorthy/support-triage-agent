Title: Refund policy

We offer a full refund on any subscription plan within 30 days of the original
purchase date, no questions asked. To request a refund, contact support with
your order number and we will process it back to your original payment method
within 5 to 7 business days.

After 30 days, subscriptions are non-refundable, but you can cancel at any time
to stop future charges. If you were charged twice for the same billing period,
that is a duplicate charge and we will always refund the extra amount in full
once verified, regardless of the 30-day window.
