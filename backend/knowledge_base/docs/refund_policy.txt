Title: Refund policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on any subscription plan. If you are not
satisfied, request a refund within 30 days of the original purchase or renewal
date and we will refund it in full, no questions asked. To request one, contact
support with your order number or the email on the account; you can also start
the request from Billing > Invoices by selecting the charge and choosing
"Request refund".

Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method and typically take
5 to 7 business days to appear, depending on your bank or card issuer. Refunds
to prepaid or expired cards may take longer and occasionally require providing an
alternate method.

After the 30-day window, subscriptions are generally non-refundable, but you can
cancel at any time to stop future charges and keep access until the end of the
period you already paid for. Annual plans past 30 days are not prorated for a
mid-term refund.

Some charges are handled separately from the 30-day rule. Duplicate charges — for
example being billed twice in the same billing period — are always refunded in
full once verified, regardless of timing. One-time hardware purchases follow the
return policy on the shipping page rather than this subscription policy. If you
opened a dispute or chargeback with your bank, we cannot issue a manual refund
for the same charge until the dispute is resolved, so please contact us first so
we can sort it out faster than the bank's process.
