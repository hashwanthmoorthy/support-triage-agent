Title: Changing your subscription plan (upgrade or downgrade)

You can change plans at any time from Billing > Plan. WidgetPro offers Free, Pro,
and Business tiers; higher tiers unlock more features, higher usage limits, and
faster support response times. A comparison of what each tier includes is shown
on the plan-selection screen before you confirm.

When you upgrade, the new features unlock immediately. We charge a prorated
amount for the remainder of the current billing period — you only pay for the
days left at the higher price — and then the full new price applies from the next
renewal. Upgrading from monthly to annual switches your renewal date to one year
out and applies the annual discount right away.

When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing
period rather than immediately, so you keep the higher-tier features you already
paid for until then. We do not issue partial refunds for downgrades; instead the
lower price simply applies to every cycle after the switch. If the lower tier has
smaller usage limits, make sure your current usage fits before the change takes
effect.

Your data, settings, and content are always preserved across plan changes in
either direction. On Business plans, adding or removing seats is also prorated:
new seats are charged for the remaining days of the period, and removed seats are
credited toward your next invoice. If a plan change does not appear to have
applied correctly, check Billing > Invoices to confirm the proration before
contacting support.
