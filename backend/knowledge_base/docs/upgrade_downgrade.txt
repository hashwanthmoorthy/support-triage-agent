Title: Upgrading or downgrading your plan

You can change plans at any time from Billing > Plan. When you upgrade, the new
features unlock immediately and we charge a prorated amount for the rest of the
current billing period, so you only pay for the days you use.

When you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing
period so you keep the higher-tier features you already paid for until then. We
do not issue partial refunds for downgrades, but the lower price applies to
every cycle after the switch. Your data is preserved across plan changes.
