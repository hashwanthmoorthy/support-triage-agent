Title: Resetting your password

If you are locked out or have forgotten your password, you can reset it yourself.
Go to the sign-in page and click "Forgot password". Enter the email address on
your account and we will send a reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes.

Open the email and click "Reset password", then choose a new password that is at
least 12 characters long. For security we never send your existing password by
email. If you do not receive the reset email within a few minutes, check your
spam folder and make sure you used the email tied to your account.
