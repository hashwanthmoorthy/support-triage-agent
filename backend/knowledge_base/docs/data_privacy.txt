Title: Data privacy and your information

We collect only the data needed to operate your account: your email, billing
details, and the content you choose to store in the product. We also collect
basic usage and device information to keep the service secure and reliable. We do
not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your private
content to train models. Our full privacy policy lists every category of data,
why we hold it, and how long we keep it.

You have the right to access and export your data. Submit a request from Settings
> Privacy > Export my data and we will email a downloadable archive, in a
machine-readable format, within 30 days — usually much sooner. The export
includes your profile, settings, and stored content.

You can also request correction or deletion of your data. Deleting your account
erases your personal data within 30 days, except records we are legally required
to retain (for example tax and fraud-prevention records). Because deletion is
irreversible, requests that combine deletion with a refund are reviewed by an
agent before anything is removed.

We use cookies for sign-in sessions and to remember your preferences; you can
manage non-essential cookies from the cookie banner or your browser settings. For
privacy questions, to object to specific processing, or to reach our data
protection team, contact support and mark the request as a privacy matter and it
will be routed appropriately. Customers in regions with laws such as GDPR or CCPA
have these same rights guaranteed under those regulations.
