Title: Data privacy and your information

We collect only the data needed to run your account: your email, billing
details, and the content you choose to store. We do not sell personal data to
third parties. Our full privacy policy explains each category of data and how
long it is kept.

You have the right to request a copy of all data we hold about you. Submit a
data export request from Settings > Privacy > Export my data and we will email a
downloadable archive within 30 days. To object to specific processing or ask a
privacy question, contact our data protection team through support.
